Responsible Entity Services

Melbourne Venture Securities operates numerous Responsible Entities. Each of these companies holds an n Financial Services Licence and is licensed, pursuant to the Corporations Act, to act as a Responsible Entity for registered managed investment schemes. One Investment Group is one of ’s leading providers of Responsible Entity services.

Trustee Services

Melbourne Venture Securities’s subsidiaries are authorised by our n Financial Services Licences to act as Trustee to unregistered managed investment schemes. Melbourne Venture Securities comprises an experienced team providing trustee services to a range of clients. Our team has a deep understanding of trust administration across a variety of trust structures and trust assets developed over more than 20 years.

Corporate Governance Services

Melbourne Venture Securities provides a wide range of corporate governance services for entities who require internal corporate compliance management. We are here to help you maintain compliance without the additional cost of permanent compliance staff or the stress of diverting key management resources to compliance tasks.

Custody Services

We offer a wide range of services to assist in the management and safeguarding of asset portfolios that can be tailored to your needs. Our reputation is built on our specialists within our business and our dedicated client relationship model. As a custodian, we hold legal title to scheme assets, ensuring they are properly segregated from the assets of other trusts.

Managed Investment Trusts

Melbourne Venture Securities’s subsidiaries are considered the leading providers of trustee and investment management services to Managed Investment Trusts (“MITs”) in . Melbourne Venture Securities provides trustee and investment management services to some of the world’s largest banks, pension funds, private equity firms and insurance companies. Assets within these MITs include real estate, infrastructure and financial assets.

Significant Investor Visa Funds

The n Federal Government’s Significant Investor Visa (“SIV”) program allows high net worth individuals to apply for permanent residency in by investing into a complying investment. Melbourne Venture Securities is the largest provider of trustee, custody, administration and registry services to SIV compliant funds in . We assist a wide range of fund managers operate SIV compliant funds including both local and offshore managers.

Fund Administration

Through its affiliate, Unity Fund Services, Melbourne Venture Securities provides independent fund accounting, administration and tax services to a diverse range of clients, from start ups to established industry heavyweights. Unity Fund Services have a dedicated and qualified team who use the best available technology and systems to deliver accurate and timely information to investment managers.

Registry Services

One Registry Services is a trusted provider of registry services for financial market participants in . As a registrar, One Registry Services offers a comprehensive range of reliable, prompt and cost effective registry solutions to unlisted companies and trusts. Ancillary services include registry management, dividend and distribution payments, AGM services, investor reporting, enquiry management and corporate actions.
